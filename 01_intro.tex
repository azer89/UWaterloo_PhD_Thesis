\mynote{
Thesis Statement (one or two sentences)
\begin{itemize}
\item What is your thesis about and what have you done?
\item If you have a hypothesis what is it?
\item How will you test (prove/disprove) your hypothesis?
\end{itemize}
}

\mynote{
Motivation
\begin{itemize}
\item Why is this problem you've worked on important
\end{itemize}
}

\mynote{
Goals / Objectives
\begin{itemize}
\item What are you trying to do and why?
\item How will you or the reader know if or when you've met your objectives?
\end{itemize}
}

\mynote{
Contributions
\begin{itemize}
\item What is new, different, better, significant?
\item Why is the world a better place because of what you've done?
\item What have you contributed to the field of research?
\item What is now known/possible/better because of your thesis?
\end{itemize}
}
